MainCrafts was born from a simple belief — great products shouldn't require a 200-person team or a $10M budget. We exist to level the playing field.

Our Story

Started in a garage.
Now powering thousands.

In 2021, our founders were frustrated. They kept seeing brilliant startup ideas die not from lack of vision — but from lack of execution speed. Talented teams wasted months on infrastructure, design systems, and tooling before writing a single line of product code.

So they built MainCrafts — a platform that strips away the overhead and hands builders a rocket ship on day one. Today, over 10,000 teams across 60+ countries use MainCrafts to go from idea to live product in days, not months.
